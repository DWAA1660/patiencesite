About Prairie Homestead

Founded in 2020, Prairie Homestead started with a simple mission: to reconnect with the land and provide our community with ethically raised, heritage breed poultry. What began as a small backyard flock has grown into a passion for preserving rare breeds and sharing the joy of homesteading.

Our Story

We believe that happy birds lay the best eggs. Our journey hasn't always been easy, but every challenge has taught us something new about resilience and care. We focus on sustainable practices that enrich the soil and provide a natural environment for our animals.

From dawn till dusk, our family works together to ensure every animal gets the attention it deserves.

Our Flocks

We specialize in Black Copper Marans, Ameraucanas, and Olive Eggers. We carefully select our breeding stock for health, temperament, and egg color. Our goal is to offer you birds that are not only productive but also beautiful additions to your backyard.

We are constantly improving our lines to ensure you get the best quality genetics available.
